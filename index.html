Biography

I have received the B.S.degree from the Schoolof Electrical Engineering and Automation, Henan Polytechnic University, JiaoZuo, China, in 2010, and received the Ph.D.degree in pattern recognition and intelligent systems from the Key Laboratory of Intelligent Perceptionand Image Understanding of Ministry of Education, International Research Center for Intelligent Perception and Computation, Joint International Research Laboratory of Intelligent Perception and Computation, Xidian University, Xi'an, China, in 2015..

My research interests include machine learning (sparse/collaborative/low-rank representation, deep learning), pattern classification (image classification/segmentation, clustering, feature selection), and time-series analysis (anomaly detection and forecast).
